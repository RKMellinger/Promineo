Welcome to our members page.

Below is a listing of our current members.

Board Members:

Baron Von Commadore Shenanigan

President and one of the founding members of the Battle Wolf, Shenanigan is the commadore of the Battle Wolf who was gifted the title of Baron with land
earnings and all privileges thereof by one of the patrons and Letter of marque issuer's,
the Duke of Milan. His highly charismatic nature can inspire any of his followers into
battle or the local pub with equal enthusiasm.

Gunpowder Gertrude

Treasurer and one of the founding members of the Battle Wolf,
Gunpowder has an eye of booty. She keeps the books balanced and the
crews of the other ships in line.


Captain Edward "Eddie" Hawkins

Vice President and one of the founding members of the Battle Wolf,
Captain Hawkins is a hard driving scoundrel who expects his crew to pull their weight.
He is also one of the best cooks in the flotilla.

Captain Charlotte McBadger-Martogg

Administrative Officer, This no-nonsense Captain is as organized as she is unpredictable. She has many faces depending
on the situation,

  • When everything goes according to plan, she is the kindest captain you could wish for.
  • If she gets too merry in the merry making, then the meaning of her McBadger clan saying "I DO WHAT I WANT." becomes clear.
  • If thinks do not go to plan and crew cannot pull their weigh, this is when the dreaded Red Charlotte comes to sew terror in everyone.

Captain Grim "Ginger-spice" Stormcloud

Sargent at Arms and one of the founding members of the Battle Wolf,
Captain Grim is as hard as they come. He expects everyone on his crew to to work almost as hard
as they play, but be warned, he once set his own ship on fire to send a warning to his enemies.


Our main patreon, er, patron:

Duca Ludovico "il Moro" Sforvza, Ducato di Milano

The reverent Duke of Milan has been a supporter of our cause for many years. As the issuer of
our letter of Marque, he has aided in our liberation of lost and/or "abandoned" high seas cargo,
and is always happy to receive our tribute.

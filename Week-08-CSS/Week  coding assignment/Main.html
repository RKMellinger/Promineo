Welcome to the Battle Wolf!

Welcome traveler to the home of the Battle Wolf. We are a non-profit 501c3 group of historical reenactors who travel around the southwest of the new world bringing enlightenment and educational fun.

  • Viking Skalds and Sagas: Nightly fireside tellings of skalds and singing of songs amist food and wine.
  • General merry making: We pass good tithings to all who pass. We offer tours of our encampment and some members even distribute our liberated wealth to passing children.
  • Giant jenga but with swords: Yes! Come play our oversized Jenga with the use of swords. To free the block you must use a rapier to remove it from the tower, only after the block has hit the ground can you use your hand.. En garde!
  • Bartering table: We offer bartering services, have an item for trade? Bring it by and see if there is something you'd be willing to trade it for. Selections change hourly so be sure to stop by often!
For further information please visit our official facebook site.
